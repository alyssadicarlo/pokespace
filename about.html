PokéSpace's Mission

Coming off of the high from his hugely successful project MySpace, Tom knew what the world was lacking. A social media platform for Pokémon. How many Pokémon did you know on MySpace? This was the question that kept Tom up at night and PokéSpace was the answer. PokéSpace is here to provide a social media platform for each and every Pokémon no matter what region they're from or Pokéball they reside in.
